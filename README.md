# 🍄 Mycro

### *Run your mushroom farm on more than a notebook.*

Grow-operations software for small **gourmet & functional mushroom farms** —
track every batch from spawn to final flush, catch contamination early, and know
your real yield, cost, and profit per block.

**🌐 Live site:** https://saschaheyer.github.io/mycro/ ·
**🧮 Free Grow Calculator:** https://saschaheyer.github.io/mycro/calculator.html

> For legal culinary & medicinal mushroom cultivation only — Mycro does not support or
> condone the cultivation of controlled substances.

## What's here
- `docs/` — the public site (hosted on GitHub Pages)
  - `index.html` — landing page
  - `calculator.html` — the free **Mushroom Grow Calculator** (substrate hydration,
    spawn ratio, yield from biological efficiency, and full cost & profit per block)
  - `substrate-calculator.html` — the free **Substrate Recipe Calculator**
  - `guides/` — cornerstone grower guides (biological efficiency, and more)
  - `styles.css` — shared styles

## The product (in build order)
1. **Free Grow Calculator** ✅ — the acquisition wedge (you're looking at it).
2. **Landing + waitlist** ✅ — validate demand, collect early-access signups.
3. **The app** (next phases) — batch grow log, contamination tracking, yield/BE
   analytics, COGS & pricing, harvest calendar, wholesale orders.

## Why
Mushroom cultivation is indoor, biological, batch-based manufacturing — not field
farming. Generic farm software doesn't model spawn ratios, substrate moisture,
biological efficiency, or contamination, which are the things that actually decide
whether a small mushroom farm makes money. Most growers run on spreadsheets. Mycro
is purpose-built for the workflow.
