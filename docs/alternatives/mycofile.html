What MycoFile is genuinely good at

We're not here to talk you out of MycoFile — it's one of the better-built grow trackers out there, and for a phone-first grower it may be the right pick. Credit where it's due:

  • Polished native apps. Real iOS and Android apps with strong reviews, designed specifically for mushroom growers — not a generic farm app bent to fit.
  • End-to-end encryption on every tier. Your data is encrypted, even on the free plan. If privacy is a priority, that's a real, concrete feature.
  • QR label printing & scanning. Print a code for each jar or bag and scan it to pull up the grow. For a physical lab that's a genuinely nice workflow a browser tab can't match.
  • A free tier, plus recipes & supplies tracking. The Hobby plan is free (up to 15 active items), and supplies/recipe tracking is included on all tiers.

If you want a native phone app with QR labels and encryption and you're comfortable installing an app and creating an account, MycoFile is an excellent pick — you can stop reading here with our blessing.
