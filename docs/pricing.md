# Pricing — Mycro

Mycro is grow-operations software for small gourmet & functional mushroom farms (oyster, lion's mane, shiitake and similar culinary/medicinal species). It runs in any web browser — nothing to install. The free tools are live today; the cloud Grower/Farm plans are in early access (active build).

All prices in USD. Cloud plans billed monthly, secure checkout by Stripe, cancel anytime. Cloud-plan prices are early-access targets and may change before launch. Last updated: 2026-06-14.

## Free — $0
- Price: **$0 / month**, no account required.
- The free **Grow Log** (batch tracker) at https://usemycro.com/grow-log.html — log batches from inoculation → colonization → fruiting → harvest, with automatic biological efficiency, days-in-stage, overdue-colonization flag, and contamination rate. Saves in the browser; exports CSV/JSON. No item cap.
- The **Grow Calculator**, **Substrate Recipe Calculator**, and **Fruiting Conditions** cheat-sheet (14 species).
- All grower guides.
- Cloud free tier (when the app launches): up to **3 active batches** with an account.

## Grower — ~$12/month (early-access target) · most popular
- Price: **~$12 / month**.
- Everything in Free, plus:
- **Unlimited** active batches.
- Full grow log with cloud sync and backup across devices.
- Contamination analytics, yield & biological-efficiency (BE) reports.
- COGS & pricing, harvest calendar, CSV export.

## Farm — ~$24/month (early-access target)
- Price: **~$24 / month**.
- Everything in Grower, plus:
- Wholesale order management.
- Pack lists & labels.
- Multi-user (whole-team access).
- Simple invoicing.

## Founding Grower — $99 one-time
- Price: **$99 one-time** (pre-order). Only 50 spots.
- Locks **lifetime founder pricing** — you never pay standard rates.
- First access the day Mycro opens, plus a direct line to the team to shape the roadmap, and a Founding Grower badge.
- This is a pre-order for software still in active build, and is **fully refundable any time before launch**.
- Reserve at https://usemycro.com/#founding
